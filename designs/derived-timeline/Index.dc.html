A timeline for a hub that never got one

Most hubs will never have an authored timeline. But many already carry dates — checklist due-dates, milestones, a pickup, the hub’s own countdown. Phase 2 projects those existing dated blocks into the same timeline card and detail — on-device, content-blind, nothing authored or invented. This delta designs only the states that make it read as derived, honestly.

01 · the cruxProvenance — say mechanics, not magic

The chip is where honesty lives

Authored timelines wear a purple “Added to this hub” chip with a sparkle — someone placed those stops on purpose. Derived timelines invented nothing, so they wear a quiet neutral chip with a calendar glyph: a plain system fact, unmistakable from authored, no “AI” overclaim. 04Render-only, everywhere

A derived stop does not notify and is never a Now item (ADR 0046, decision 2). There is no bell, no reminder chip, no “notify me” anywhere in the derived card or detail — the copy says plainly “it just shows what’s already here.” The dates were already visible in their own blocks; the timeline only rearranges them in time.
