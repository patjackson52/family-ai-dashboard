Authored says magic. Derived must say mechanics.

An authored timeline was written to the hub on purpose — its chip earns a colored, purposeful voice. A derived timeline invented nothing: it is a plain layout of dated blocks that were already in the hub. Its chip has to read as a quiet system fact, never a badge that implies someone (or an AI) built something. Two rules: visually unmistakable from authored, and no overclaim.
