Triggers, where they live — on the content

A trigger is metadata Claude attaches to a card or Hub block. The device matches it on-device — time against the clock, place against your saved places — and nothing about where you are or when leaves the phone. The chip is the affordance: a calm when for time, a near for place, and an emphasized state the moment a place trigger is live.
