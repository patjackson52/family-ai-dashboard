# Agent: adversarial-skeptic / red team (2026-06-18)

**Verdict: KILL / pivot-or-rescope as a business. Survivable only as a
deliberate, scoped LEARNING-LAB with revenue expectations ~zero and child
accounts dropped.**

**Fatal risks (any one disqualifying as a business):**
1. **Google already shipped the core wedge** — Gemini Daily Brief curates
   Gmail+Calendar+Tasks, demoed family school-email digests, free-in-bundle
   in a sub users already hold. (gemini.google/overview/daily-brief)
2. **Gmail read = mandatory recurring paid CASA audit; self-scan dead** —
   permanent fixed cost (~$500-1k+/yr low end) before a dollar of revenue.
   Cut Gmail → the distinctive email-driven actions vanish. Lose-lose.
   (developers.google.com restricted-scope-verification)
3. **Per-member minor logins = COPPA + state App-Store-Accountability laws +
   Apple Kids-Category link-out ban** — a legal wall, not a feature. The
   flagship Instacart action is both un-monetizable (no entity, 30-40d
   Tastemakers approval) and non-compliant behind a kids gate.
   (ftc.gov COPPA FAQ; loeb.com app-store age laws; developer.apple.com)
4. **"Smart actions" = template-bounded GPT wrapper over public APIs** — no
   proprietary data, no network effect, no switching cost; clonable in a
   weekend by anyone, including the platform owner who already has the data.

**Internal inconsistencies flagged:** (a) the dogfood content-API MVP
validates *operator enthusiasm*, not the recurring-WTP the operator's own
confidence bar demands — orthogonal to the gate; (b) compliance is named the
top hard-stop yet the chosen segment (minors + Gmail + commerce deep-links)
maximizes compliance exposure.

**Cheapest kill-checks (~$0):** run Gemini Daily Brief's school-email→family-
digest flow yourself; read restricted-scope + CASA docs; read COPPA amended-
rule + Apple Kids guidelines; skim Instacart IDP approval gate; 5 cold parent
interviews ("what would you cancel to pay for this?").

Sources: gemini.google; developers.google.com OAuth; ftc.gov COPPA; FTC
amended-rule; developer.apple.com guidelines; docs.instacart.com.
